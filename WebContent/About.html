    About Us

    Our Mission



    Our mission is to create a measurable, sustainable and profitable link between pharmacies, couriers and customers. Our operation is also to bring convenience to customers by filtering their
    needs according to the type of payment they want and the type of delivery schedule that fits their respective timeframes.
                       ● To create connections            
    ● To deliver on time
           ● To promote efficiency

    Our Vision


    We hope that our system will be allied with a multitude of established pharmacies
    and delivery services that ensure a long-term partnership of delivering the fastest and
    most efficient list of products in the near future.


    Products and Services



    Our online system offers the following services:
          ● Order and delivery status for customers
    ● Verification of prescription legitimacy
          ● Ordering and delivery for over the counter medication
           ● Notification to couriers on orders to be delivered
    ● Single transaction deliveries from the nearest branch of the customers choosing
    ● Senior and PWD discounts
